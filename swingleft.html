I would love to work
For Swing Left

I spent 5 hours building this 3D model of your logo just to prove that point. I may have also spent 2 hours trying to learn how to perform a smoke simulation (and failing) so I could make the swoosh over the 'i' more exact, but that was a bit above my level of expertise.

I have been passionate about politics since the presidential election in 2016. That is what started me on my journey to become an empassioned political pundit over the previous four years, at least in my own social circle. Over that time I have watched our country be continually threatened by ever growing partisanship. So to me, 'Swing Left' is not just a political organization but a necessary action we must take if we are going to be able to preserve not only life in America as we have known it in the past, but to better the lives of all people in this country for the future.

So, quite honestly, I do not have much to show in the way of written documents that express my intuition and knowledge of the world. What I do have is this website, its content, and the 3D model that I built in the past few days when I received the email from Libby so I could prove I am willing to put in the work for your organization. Nothing is more imporant to me than making a positive change in this country and this world. The ability for me to truly make that happen starts right here with this part time opportunity.

You can visit the links below to explore more of the content I placed on this site as well. I look forward to hearing back from you.
